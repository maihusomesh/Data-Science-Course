About Me

Hello! My name is Somesh Nawani, and I am a data science student at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (online).

Why I Created This Website

I created this website as a personal project to enhance my own learning journey. Like many students, I found that following a long video series could be challenging without a clear, interactive way to track progress. My goal was to build a tool that would not only help me stay organized and motivated but could also serve as a valuable, gamified resource for the entire community.

This website is my way of giving back and making the path to becoming a data scientist a little more engaging for everyone. I hope you find it as useful as I do!
